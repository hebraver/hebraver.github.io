Future Careers: Architecture

I am interested in becoming an architect because I like math and It seems like an cool career. An architect works on public or private projects and designs both indoor and outdoor spaces. Architects can be commissioned to design anything from a single room to an entire complex of buildings. Architects discuss with clients the objectives, requirements, and budget of a project. In Chicago, there is a salary low of about 58k, a high of about 163k, and an average of about 97k. To become an architect, you need a professional degree in architecture from a National Architectural Accrediting Board (NAAB) accredited program. This degree can be a Bachelor of Architecture (B. Arch) or a Master of Architecture. The traits of an architect are the following: design skills and knowledge, knowledge of building and construction, to be thorough and pay attention to detail, thinking and reasoning skills, customer service skills, excellent verbal communication skills, analytical thinking skills, and the ability to use your initiative.

University of Illinois Chicago (UIC)

I would like to go to the University of Illinois Chicago because it is a good school with a high acceptance rate and it is close to home. The tuition to get in is $31k before aid and $11k after aid. The requirements to apply are that you must submit $60 application, high school transcript, and (optional) submit ACT or SAT scores. And then some helpful amenities are all rooms come fully furnished and include all basic utilities, free laundry and 24-hour security measures.

University of Notre Dame

I would like to go to University of Notre Dame because it has a good architectural program and it is considered on of the best colleges you could attend. Tuition costs 78k before aid and 32k after aid. Requirements to apply to Notre Dame are your academic performance in high school, high school academic requirements, standardized tests scores, letter(s) of evaluation, and an Essay. And after thoroughly searching, the main amenitie tied to Notre Dame is it's beautiful and scenic campus.
